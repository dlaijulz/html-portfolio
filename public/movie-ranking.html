The Best Movies According to Julius

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time.

Saving Private Ryan

This is my No. 1 favorite movie ever. Not only is it based on a true story, but the cinematography makes it more realistic. It is a breathtaking, heart-wrenching masterpiece that takes me to reality through the horrors of World War II.

Sisu

This is not a very different story. But this captured my spirit of how an ex-soldier exiled itself to escape the war and wanted to live in peace despite the ongoing war with the Nazis. This is an absolutely gripping, powerful, and emotional movie. Showing the strength of the human will to survive and to protect its precious property.

Braveheart

The best movie that has ever been made in the history of Scotland. The portrayal of emotions of suffering and courage to love was clearly depicted. The battle scenes are brutal and groundbreaking. Epic!
